[zkImagine](https://element.market/collections/zkimagine?search%5Btoggles%5D%5B0%5D=ALL) is a NFT collection on ZKsync Era mainnet. All images are generated with Heurist Imagine. The supply is unlimited. Creators can launch sub-collections from the zkImagine collection, and turn your creativity into your customized NFT collection.

### Mint Fee

* Regular mint: 0.001 ETH

* Mint with referral: 0.0009 ETH (for every referred mint, 0.0001 ETH revenue goes to the referrer)

* Free mint: We give free mint whitelists to Heurist community members, such as early miners, social media campaign participants, Discord roles, and ambassadors.
